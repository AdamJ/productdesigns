## üìù Brand Voice

### Tone Attributes
- **Energetic** - Active, dynamic, exciting
- **Confident** - Assured, professional, capable
- **Innovative** - Forward-thinking, creative
- **Accessible** - Friendly, helpful, clear
- **Playful** - Game-inspired, fun (but not childish)

### Writing Style
- Use active voice
- Short, punchy sentences
- Technical but not jargon-heavy
- Encouraging and empowering
- Clear and direct

### Examples

**Good:**
- "Strike the right balance with ArcLight Pulse"
- "Feel the energy in every interaction"
- "Pulse brings your designs to life"

**Avoid:**
- "Utilize the ArcLight Pulse framework" (too formal)
- "Super cool awesome design!" (too casual)
- "Leverage synergies..." (corporate jargon)
